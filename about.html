About

I like working where machine learning meets systems: vector search, evaluation pipelines, backend services, and interfaces that make AI features easy for real users to trust.

Outside of class, I experiment with games, web apps, and new AI frameworks. I enjoy taking messy problem spaces and designing small, focused tools that make them feel manageable.
