About

Hi, I'm Atharva Rai — a junior at the University of Michigan studying Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics. I love solving problems through a mix of AI, systems engineering, and thoughtful product design. Over the past few years, I’ve worked on projects that combine machine learning, backend infrastructure, and real-world usability, and I’m most excited when I’m turning complex technical ideas into something practical and impactful.

This past summer, I helped build a Retrieval-Augmented Generation chatbot at Blue Cross Blue Shield of Michigan, which allowed employees to search thousands of policy documents instantly instead of digging through them manually. Seeing how that tool immediately improved everyday workflows made me realize how powerful applied intelligence can be when it’s built with users in mind.

Outside of internships, I’ve spent time building full-stack and backend systems, experimenting with databases, authentication flows, real-time features, and APIs. These projects have shown me how much impact comes from pairing solid engineering with intuitive design.

I’d describe myself as someone who embraces new challenges, enjoys building things end-to-end, and is always looking for opportunities to create meaningful impact with technology.
